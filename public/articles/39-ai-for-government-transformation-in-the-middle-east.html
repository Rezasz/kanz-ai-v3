Government Velocity

Middle East governments are moving faster on AI than any other region.

From the UAE's AI minister appointment in 2017 to Stargate UAE and Saudi's NSDAI today, the region treats AI as a national strategic asset. Public-sector AI here is not catching up — it is setting the global bar.

03The Article

Middle East governments have positioned AI as a national strategic asset, with the UAE leading globally and Saudi Arabia moving rapidly. AI is now the operating layer of government transformation — and the bar for citizen excellence, sovereignty, and trust is among the highest anywhere.

Four arenas, one transformation.

Citizen experience, operational efficiency, public safety and cities, sovereignty and trust. Each is funded; each is governed; together they define government in 2026.

Why has the Middle East moved faster than other regions?

Through the UAE AI Charter principles, public-sector deployment standards, and increasingly visible audit trails.
